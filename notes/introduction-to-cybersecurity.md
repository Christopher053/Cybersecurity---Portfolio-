# Introduction to Cybersecurity

## What is Cybersecurity?
Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks. These attacks may aim to access, change, or destroy sensitive information or disrupt normal business operations.

## Why Cybersecurity is Important
- Protects sensitive data
- Prevents unauthorized access
- Ensures system availability
- Reduces financial and reputational loss

## Core Areas of Cybersecurity
- Network Security
- Application Security
- Information Security
- Operational Security
- Incident Response

## Common Cyber Threats
- Malware
- Phishing
- Ransomware
- Social engineering
- Unauthorized access
